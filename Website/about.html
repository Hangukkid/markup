About!

This spectacular masterpiece was created for YorkUHacks 2018 by Mike Shi, Matthew Won, Jeff Lo, Sooyeon Cho, and Shayan Hz


Every day, teachers spend hours and hours afterschool grading their students' tests and quizzes. Not only do the teachers suffer, but the students as well, beause their teachers are too tired. MarkUp aims to change that, by providing a platform for teachers to create and automatically grade work so they can spend more time relaxing and being better prepared for the next day.
